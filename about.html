About Pet Calculators

Welcome to Pet Calculators, a free online resource designed to help pet owners better understand and care for their dogs and cats.

Our goal is to provide simple, accurate, and easy-to-use calculators that help answer common pet care questions, including age conversion, food intake, calorie needs, water intake, pregnancy timelines, and growth estimates.

Our Mission

We believe pet care information should be accessible to everyone. By offering free calculators and educational articles, we aim to help pet owners make informed decisions about their pets' health and well-being.

What We Offer

  • Dog and cat age calculators
  • Food intake calculators
  • Water intake calculators
  • Calorie requirement calculators
  • Puppy growth and weight estimation tools
  • Educational pet care articles

Important Disclaimer

The calculators and information provided on this website are intended for educational and informational purposes only. They should not be considered veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

If you have concerns about your pet's health, nutrition, or medical condition, please consult a licensed veterinarian.

Continuous Improvement

We are continuously adding new calculators, articles, and resources to improve the website and provide additional value for pet owners around the world.
